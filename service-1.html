Licensing Information

Understanding Power Apps licensing is crucial for planning your project budget. Licensing primarily depends on the data sources (connectors) your app needs:

  • Standard Connectors: If your app only uses standard connectors (like SharePoint, OneDrive, and basic Microsoft 365 services), usage is typically included at no extra cost in your existing Microsoft 365 Business or Enterprise plan.
  • Premium Connectors: To connect to premium sources like SQL Server, Dataverse, Salesforce, or custom APIs, you will need a standalone Power Apps license.
  • Per App Plan: Ideal for businesses that want to license users for specific apps only. Costs roughly $5 per user/app/month.
  • Per User Plan: Best if your users need access to multiple premium apps. Costs roughly $20 per user/month.
