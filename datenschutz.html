Data Privacy Policy

1. Introduction and Overview

This Data Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your personal data. The protection of your personal data is very important to us. We process your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Responsible Party (Data Controller)

Name: Julius Bruch
Email: info@jb-solutions.digital

3. Data Processing

Personal data is only collected and processed to the extent necessary for the provision of services and to comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Contact Form and Email

When you contact us via our contact form or email, the data you provide (e.g., name, email address, message) will be processed solely for the purpose of answering your inquiry. Your data will not be passed on to third parties. Your personal data will be deleted after your request has been processed and no legal retention periods apply.

5. Website Hosting & Contact Form Processor

Our website and contact form are hosted and processed by a hosting provider (such as Netlify). Additionally, we use the service "Resend" to send automated confirmation emails when you submit the contact form. To deliver this email, your name, email address, and message are transmitted to Resend via a secure API. The hoster may also collect technical data about visitors in log files (e.g., IP address, browser type, time of request).

6. Local Storage and Privacy-Friendly Analytics

This website does not use cookies for tracking. We only use your browser's local storage to save your visual theme and language preferences locally. To understand website traffic without compromising your privacy, we use Simple Analytics. It collects no personal data, uses no cookies, and is fully GDPR-compliant.

7. External Links & Social Media

Our website contains simple links to external websites and social media platforms (e.g., LinkedIn, GitHub). We do not use social media plugins that automatically transmit data. When you click on an external link, you leave our website and are subject to the data protection practices of the external provider.

8. Your Rights

According to the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request information about what personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can request that incorrect data be corrected.
  • Right to erasure: You can request that your data be deleted ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request that your data be provided to you in a structured format.
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your data.

9. Data Security

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

10. Contact Information for Data Protection

If you have any questions about this Data Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:
Email: info@jb-solutions.digital

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available on this website.

12. Supervisory Authority

If you believe that our processing of your personal data violates the GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority in your country.
