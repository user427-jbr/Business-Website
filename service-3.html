Licensing Information

SharePoint Online is one of the most accessible platforms because it is already heavily seeded within the Microsoft 365 ecosystem:

  • Included in M365: Most Business Basic, Standard, Premium, and Enterprise (E3/E5) plans include full SharePoint Online access. You generally do not need to buy standalone SharePoint licenses for internal employees.
  • Storage Limits: A tenant typically receives 1 TB of base storage, plus 10 GB per licensed user. If your organization stores massive amounts of data, you may need to purchase 'Extra File Storage' add-ons.
  • External Guests: Sharing SharePoint sites or documents with external clients or partners is usually free and covered under Microsoft's guest licensing models, making it a great portal for external collaboration.
